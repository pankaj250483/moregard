Business Model

Building companies well positioned for the future

  • 01 Thematic and sector-based approach.

    Temptation Capital applies a thematic approach in deal sourcing. Guided by underlying macro trends, Temptation Capital targets high-quality companies with significant sustainable growth potential in attractive industries with secular growth drivers and strong downside protection.

  • 02 Locals-with-locals.

    Temptation Capital believes that local knowledge, local business relationships, local presence and access to local deal flow are all critical to securing a competitive edge in private markets.

  • 03 Digitalization.

    Leveraging world-class industrial expertise has always been part of Temptation Capital DNA and exploiting digital opportunities is critical for future-proofing the Temptation Capital funds’ portfolio companies.
